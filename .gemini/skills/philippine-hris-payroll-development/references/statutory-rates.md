# Statutory Rates (2026)

## SSS (Social Security System)

- **Total Contribution Rate**: 15% of Monthly Salary Credit (MSC)
- **Employee Share**: 5% of MSC
- **Employer Share**: 10% of MSC
- **Minimum MSC**: ₱5,000 (for salary ≤ ₱5,250)
- **Maximum MSC**: ₱35,000 (for salary > ₱34,750)
- **Incremental Steps**: ₱500 per bracket

## PhilHealth

- **Total Premium Rate**: 5% of monthly basic salary
- **Employee Share**: 2.5%
- **Employer Share**: 2.5%
- **Minimum Base Salary**: ₱10,000 (Premium: ₱500)
- **Maximum Base Salary**: ₱100,000 (Premium: ₱5,000)

## Pag-IBIG (HDMF)

- **Salary ≤ ₱1,500**: 1% employee, 2% employer
- **Salary > ₱1,500**: 2% employee, 2% employer
- **Maximum Contribution**: ₱200 each for employee and employer (capped at ₱10,000 base salary)
