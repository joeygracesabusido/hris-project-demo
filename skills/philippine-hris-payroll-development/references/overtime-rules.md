# Overtime and Holiday Rules

Based on the Philippine Labor Code.

## Overtime Rates

- **Ordinary Day Overtime**: 125% of hourly rate (for work beyond 8 hours)
- **Rest Day / Special Day**: 130% for first 8 hours
- **Rest Day / Special Day Overtime**: 169% (130% × 130%) for work beyond 8 hours
- **Regular Holiday**: 200% for first 8 hours
- **Regular Holiday Overtime**: 260% (200% × 130%) for work beyond 8 hours
- **Night Shift Differential**: Additional 10% for work between 10 PM and 6 AM.

## Holiday Types

- **Regular Holidays**: New Year's Day, Maundy Thursday, Good Friday, Eid'l Fitr, etc.
- **Special Non-Working Holidays**: Ninoy Aquino Day, All Saints' Day, Feast of the Immaculate Conception, etc.
